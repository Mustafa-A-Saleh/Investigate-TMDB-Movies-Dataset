Q3: Do higher revenues mean higher profits?¶

To answer this question, we add a new column 'profit' equals the difference between 'revenue_adj' and 'budget_adj'

In [241]:

In [242]:

plt.title("Revenues compared to Profits")
plt.xlabel("Revenues in US Dollar")
plt.ylabel("Profits in US Dollar");

Previous plot shows that increasing in revenues has a positive correlation with profits

Conclusions¶

After going through this analysis process, starting by assessing and cleaning the dataset (removing unnecessary columns, elimnating unnecessary data and duplicate values). Also, we explored the dataset and reached the following results using statistics and graphs:


1- Steven Spielberg generated the highest revenues with almost 15 billion dolars.
2- Drama movies have the highest revenues, and Foreign movies come last.
3- Higher profits is associated with higher revenues.
4- Most popular genres are Adventure, Science Fiction and Fantasy.
5- Too short and too long movies don't gain much popularity among people.

Limitations:¶


1- Assigning mean values instead of zeros for ('revenue_adj', 'budget_adj', 'runtime') columns may alter the results.
2- Eliminating duplicates and unknown values costed us about 10% of the dataset which may cause a slight change in the conclusion .
